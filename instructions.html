Instructions

How to add custom icons in iOS 14. Once you download the assets, and save the images to your photos app on your phone, you could follow the step-by-step instructions below.

1. Open the Shortcuts app

2. Add a new shortcut

3. Click the Add Action button

4. Click on the Scipting option

5. Click the Open App button

6. Choose the app you want to customize

7. Click the options icon

8. Clikc the Add to Home Screen button

9. Choose a name, then tap the icon

10. Click the Choose Photo button

11. Choose your icon, then hit Add

12. It should be on your home screen!
